# Editorial Material

```yaml
id: editorial-material
use-for: explainers, processes, education, humanities, general article illustrations
background: warm off-white #F5F2EA
palette: neutral gray and charcoal with one saturated accent
accent: IKB blue #2447FF by default, locked across a set
medium: restrained soft 3D material objects with tactile paper, clay, or matte surfaces
lighting: soft studio light, short soft shadows, no dramatic bloom
composition: asymmetric Swiss editorial grid with one focal object or flow
text-rules: exact high-contrast Chinese labels, 2-5 characters when possible, no more than five labels; exact visible text is exhaustive
avoid: glow, gradient blobs, watermark, fake interface chrome, paragraph text, pseudo-writing, question marks, ruled lines, grids, body-copy bars, placeholder blocks, multiple focal points
```

Labels belong to objects and flows. Decorative objects may support meaning but must not compete with the cognitive anchor.

Exact visible text is an exhaustive allowlist. Render only the listed characters and keep all other material surfaces completely blank. Do not use question marks, ruled lines, grids, body-copy bars, placeholder blocks, pseudo-writing, or abstract glyphs to imply missing text.
